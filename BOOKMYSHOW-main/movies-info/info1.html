Kalki 2898AD

Overview

Kalki 2898AD is an upcoming Indian sci-fi film directed by Nag Ashwin. The movie features a star-studded cast including Prabhas, Deepika Padukone, Kamal Haasan, Amitabh Bachchan, Disha Patani, and Pasupathy. Produced by Vyjayanthi Movies, the film is set in a futuristic world and is anticipated to be one of the major releases of the year.
